3. Q&A

Q：When you forget the password, how to do ?

A：Please contact your admin, and reset your password.

Q：Why is the picture or chart displayed correctly?

A：Please enter the Ctrl+Shift+Delete to clean the cache. If it doesn’t work, please contact admin.

Q：When you remote a mobile, the mobile is disconnected and how to do?

A：The mobile is disconnected because the USB. You can refresh, go back the Mobiles and choose again. The another way is to disconnect the USB and connect it again.
